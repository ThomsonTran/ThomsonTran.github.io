About Big John’s hand-crafted beef jerky

Big John’s Beef Jerky is hand-crafted beef jerky made from solid strips of premium quality top round beef. Our beef jerky is slow smoked over real burning hickory before it’s hand-carved into delicious, easy to eat Strips or super Thick Slabs. 100% money back guarantee on all beef jerky orders.
